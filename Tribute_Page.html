Louis Pasteur

French Chemist and Microbiologist

Louis Pasteur

  • 1822 - Born in Dole, Jura, France
  • 1895 - dies at the age of 72

☛ Known for creating the first vaccines for rabies, Cholera vaccine, Anthrax vaccines and Pasteurization

☛ Developed the process of Pasteurization which is used to kill any microorganisms in beverages

The steps of Pasteurization are:

  1. Heat a beverage to boiling temperatures
  2. The boiling water will kill any bacteria in the drink
  3. Keep the drink sealed to prevent any new bacteria from contaminating the drink
  4. Once this is done, the drink will be free from (live) bacteria as long as the liquid remains sealed.
